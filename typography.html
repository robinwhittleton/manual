Typography

For now mostly copy and paste from the existing typography manual. BUT we want to merge some details from the semantics manual too! Review both and see what we can merge in here.

  • Spacing (no double space, indentation rules)
  • Names and titles
    • Capitalization
    • Italics or quote
    • Other rules (remove archaic double quotes, etc.)
  • Capitalization
    • In titles
    • In proper names
  • Italics
    • When to use vs quotes
    • Other rules
    • Latinisms (new: list of latinisms to italicize vs not)
  • Punctuation (quotation marks, ellipses, periods within quotation marks, dashes, etc.)
  • Numbers and math
  • Miscellenea like times, chemicals, abbreviations, initialisms, etc.

Misc:

- Do not use apostrophes to pluralize numbers or abbreviations. For example, "The 1800s", "IOUs". Note that apostrophes are still used for possessive: "The MP's daughter"
